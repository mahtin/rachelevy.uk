To mark Holocaust Memorial Day 2003, I was interviewed by Zara Bishop of the South London Press.
This was one of my eariest inerviews by the press.
I had only be talking about my experiences for a handful of years.
The article tells my story in detail.

The South London Press article opened with this paragraph:

>	The horrors of the Holocaust are something few of us can properly imagine.
>	As a 14-year-old girl, a now-retired dressmaking teacher who worships at Catford Synagogue survived THREE concentration camps.
>	Her parents and three of her four siblings did not live to tell their stories.
